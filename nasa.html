01 — Spec Campaign

NASA × Ralph Lauren
Black Label — Artemis

Fashion Editorial  ·  Kennedy Space Center  ·  AI Production  ·  2025

A complete fashion editorial
at the most restricted location on earth.

The brief was simple: what if Ralph Lauren Black Label partnered with NASA on the Artemis program — a prestige collection for the people who build missions? The Artemis patch, the wool overcoat, the hand-polished dress shoes on launch tower grating.

No location permit. No casting director. No production crew. No travel budget. One creative director, a clear visual brief, and a 7-layer AI direction system. Nine campaign-ready images delivered as a complete fashion editorial.

Project Type Spec Campaign — Fashion Editorial
Brand NASA × Ralph Lauren Black Label
Location Kennedy Space Center (AI-directed)
Deliverables 9-image campaign, print & digital ready
Tools Gemini Flash · CSG PRO Framework
Year 2025
Creative Process
01
Concept & Brief
Established the brand collision — prestige menswear meets space program. Defined the emotional territory: gravity, precision, legacy. The Artemis mission patch as the product hero.
02
Location Direction
Mapped KSC landmarks — the VAB, launch pad 39B, the crawler path, the access tower grating. Each location chosen for its visual weight and what it communicates about the brand.
03
Shot Architecture
Built a 9-shot sequence — hero editorial, wide establishing, character studies, detail work, ensemble. Each frame directed with specific lens specs, lighting conditions, and talent direction.
04
Production
Executed through the CSG PRO 7-layer system — Scene Foundation through Render Parameters. Character consistency maintained across all nine frames without a single re-cast.
Campaign Hero — Artemis patch detail, launch pad 39B 01 / 06
Establishing Shot — The approach, crawler path, SLS at golden hour 02 / 06
Character Studies — Pre-launch transport / VAB Door 7 03–04 / 06
Detail — Hand-polished Black Label oxfords, launch access tower grating 05 / 06
Complete Campaign — 9 images, print and digital ready 06 / 06
