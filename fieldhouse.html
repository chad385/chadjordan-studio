05 — Editorial Direction

The Field House Series
Functional Sanctuary

The Pivot  ·  Concept & Copy  ·  Material Language  ·  2025

Design that remembers
how to breathe.

The Field House Series is an editorial concept developed for The Pivot — a complete visual and written brand world built around the idea of functional sanctuary design. Not minimalism. Enoughness.

The brief called for a mood system that could anchor a design philosophy in something felt rather than explained. The output: a complete Vol. 01 editorial with original copy, a material language system built around Oak, Earth, Textile, and Fire, and a set of individual material studies that make the abstract concrete.
