Questions

Frequently asked.

What counts as "one request"?
A single campaign concept — a series of images, a batch grid, a product swap across an existing campaign, a social cut-down. If it's one creative direction, it's one request.
How fast is turnaround, really?
2–5 business days for most requests. Larger batches — full seasonal campaigns, multi-market rollouts — take longer and get scoped up front.
What if I need revisions?
Revisions are part of the request. Within the same creative direction, revise as needed until it lands.
Who owns the work?
You do. Full rights transfer on delivery.
What happens if I pause?
Your queue holds. Pick up where you left off when you unpause. No re-onboarding.
Is there a contract?
No. Month-to-month. Cancel from the dashboard anytime.
Why subscription instead of project-by-project?
Because regional brands have continuous creative needs, and project pricing doesn't match that rhythm. Subscription does.
Who is this not for?
One-off projects. Brands without an active product line. Anyone who needs in-person production days or traditional photo shoots. The model is built for ongoing volume, not single deliverables.
