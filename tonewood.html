03 — Case Study

Tonewood Brewing
Hyper-Local Campaign

Product Localization  ·  Batch Creation  ·  Social Content  ·  2025

One product.
An entire city as the shoot location.

Tonewood Brewing is a New Jersey craft brewery with deep Philly roots. The brief: build a hyper-local campaign that places the Fuego IPA in the moments and locations where their audience actually lives — Girard Ave in Fishtown, the Philly skyline at dusk, Reading Terminal Market, the Art Museum steps.

No production days. No location scouts. No travel. Starting from a single can image, this system generated a complete multi-market social campaign, lifestyle photography, a simulated Instagram feed, and a technical schematic — all from a single product reference and a set of location prompts.

Project Type Hyper-Local Product Campaign
Brand Tonewood Brewing — Fuego IPA
Market Philadelphia / South Jersey
Deliverables Lifestyle, social, grid content, schematic
Tools Gemini Flash · CSG PRO Framework
Year 2025
Creative Process
01
Product Reference
Established the Fuego can as the consistent visual anchor — loaded as a reference image to maintain label accuracy, colorway, and proportions across every generated scene.
02
Hyper-Localization
Prompted specific Philadelphia locations with the can as reference — Girard Ave in Fishtown, rooftop decks with the skyline, Reading Terminal Market, the Liberty Bell, the Art Museum steps.
03
Batch Production
Scaled to batch grid creation — 9 and 12-image grids across Philadelphia landmarks, people, and environments. Same product, infinite contexts. A full social content library in a single session.
04
Full Deliverable Suite
Extended beyond photography into a simulated Instagram feed with captions and engagement, and a technical product schematic — demonstrating the full range of AI creative output from one brief.
Lifestyle Hero — Philly rooftop, golden hour, product forward 01 / 05
Hyper-Local — Girard Ave, Fishtown / Ambient rooftop lifestyle 02–03 / 05
Batch Creation — 9 dramatic scenes, Philadelphia landmarks, single prompt session 04 / 05
Full Campaign Grid — 12 images, Liberty Bell to Schuylkill River 05 / 05
Beyond Photography
When the system goes beyond the brief.

A technical can schematic — complete with callouts for pull-tab, polymer coating, aluminum alloy body, and offset lithography label — generated from the same product reference. This isn't a photography tool. It's a complete creative production system.
